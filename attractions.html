ATTRACTIONS

Discover Banff's top attractions and historic landmarks.

Banff Castle

A former medieval fortress, now a community and arts venue hosting events and workshops.

The Museum

Home to historical artifacts, offering insights into Banff's cultural evolution.

Golf Range

Duff House Royal Golf Club offers a well-maintained course with stunning scenery.

The Smiddy

A former blacksmith's workshop showcasing Banff's industrial past.

Gardens/The Vinery

Historic gardens and a restored vinery in the heart of Banff.

Temple of Venus

An 18th-century folly offering panoramic views of Banff and its coastline.

Macduff Marine Aquarium

Showcasing marine life from Scotland's Moray Firth.

Bridge of Alvah

A historic single-arch bridge spanning the dramatic River Deveron gorge.

Spotty Bag Shop

A beloved shop offering a variety of goods, from souvenirs to household items.
