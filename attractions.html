Banff Castle

Originally a medieval fortress, Banff Castle has undergone various transformations over the centuries. Today, it serves as a community and arts venue, hosting events and workshops

The Banff Museum houses a collection of local artifacts and exhibits that chronicle the area's history, providing visitors with a deeper understanding of the town's cultural evolution.

Temple of Venus

Perched on a hill overlooking Banff, the Temple of Venus is an 18th-century folly. This small, circular structure offers panoramic views of the town and coastline, serving as a charming historical landmark.

Spotty bag shop

A unique shopping destination in Banff, the Spotty Bag Shop is known for its wide array of products, ranging from household items to gifts, making it a popular spot for both locals and tourists.

Banff is a small, quaint town located in northeast Scotland, known for its fishing heritage, scenic golf courses, rich history, and friendly community.
